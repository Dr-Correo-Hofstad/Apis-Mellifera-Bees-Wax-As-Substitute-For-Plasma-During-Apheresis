APIS+ Sterile Plasma: The Next Evolution in Blood Product Development
=====================================================================

### **A New Standard for Global Health**

VirusTC is proud to announce the next step in the revolution of biomedical and blood product development: **APIS+ Sterile Fresh Frozen Plasma (FFP)**. Moving beyond the limitations and risks of traditional human-donor plasma, APIS+ represents a paradigm shift toward sterile, plant-based, and apiary-derived synthetic blood products.

This development means the world finally has access to a **Sterile Fresh Frozen Plasma** supply that eliminates the infection risks inherent in human-to-human donation. By utilizing organic, non-GMO beeswax and plant-based formulas, we have created a "farm-to-clinic" sterile product that has never been inside another human host, ensuring zero risk of cross-contamination or viral transmission.

### **The Teaching of Dr. Hofstad: Redefining Human Fluids**

Dr. Hofstad (USMC CMC Correo Hofstad), alongside USMC CMC Melody Bouck, has fundamentally reshaped our understanding of human plasma and body fluids. Through his research, we have learned that the biological functions of human plasma---coagulation, transport, and defense---can be mirrored and enhanced by nature's own engineers: *Apis mellifera* (honeybees).

Dr. Hofstad taught us to deconstruct plasma into its functional components and rebuild it better:

-   **Coagulation Re-imagined:** He demonstrated that just as human fibrinogen oxidizes to patch wounds, **phospholipase** in APIS+ oxidizes with hydrogen peroxide to create a viscous "biowax" seal, effectively stopping bleeding without the need for human proteins.

-   **Energy & "Flight Electrolytes":** He revealed that the electrolytes powering bee flight could be converted into clinical defense medications. APIS+ utilizes these "Flight Electrolytes" (including healthy sodium, potassium, and calcium) to increase patient biodensity and energy levels during oncology treatments.

-   **Immune Defense:** By replacing human immunoglobulins with high-alkalinity solutions and hydrogen peroxide derived from bee populations, Dr. Hofstad created a product that actively redoxes cancer and virus cells in red blood components.

### **Clinical Specifications: APIS+**

APIS+ is a clinical plasma formula designed for use with **Haemonetics brand Apheresis machines**. It serves as a direct substitute for natural FFP in the treatment of oncology and pathology patients.

-   **Composition:** A vegetarian, plant-based product containing animal components concentrated from organic beeswax.

-   **Sterility:** Manufactured via a centrifugal process where the heat generated by bees helps sterilize the product, ensuring a food-grade, clinical-grade standard.

-   **Active Ingredients:** Includes Glucose oxidase, acid phosphatase, phospholipase, and Major Royal Jelly Proteins (MRJPs) such as Royalactin.

-   **Availability:** Currently available by trial at **Fred Hutchinson Cancer Center** as part of Operation Cancer Moonshot.
